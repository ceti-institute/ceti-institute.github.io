We think of projects as drafts, that may or may not be revisited in the future depending on community inclination and need. Our projects emphasize interdisciplinary creative experimentation and participatory research around emergent technology and social impact. They are intended to spark critical discourse bridging art, technology, and social/systemic change, revealing, subverting, and reimagining the implications and possibilities of these emergent technologies.

Most importantly, they are a challenge and response to the question: Who has access to and voice/agency/power in this technology landscape as a creator, innovator, decision-maker, and leader?

We expect participants of our programs to abide by CETI Code of Conduct.
