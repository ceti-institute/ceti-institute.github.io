Enchanted Tech 2022

Summer Festival

Join the NE STEAM Coalition and CETI at RAHS for an exciting cutting-edge summer of...

Enchanted Objects, Murals, and Storytelling

A creative exploration of art, geometry, music, physics, technology to create magical objects and murals that delight and respond, and that act as portals to other experiences that reveal hidden stories. We'll create a tour of significant places, images, and sculptures in the Albina neighbourhood, using interactive murals and augmented storytelling using technology as a tool for imagination, to amplify our voices, to subvert power, and to reclaim our streetscapes as spaces of future hope and community.

Over the course of 6 weeks, we will learn to make augmented stickers and buttons, markers for delightful and secret experiences through coding, techniques of fabrication, through workshops, making sessions, show-and-tell from inspiring speakers locally and nationally, field trips to local creative organizations, mentored collaborative projects, and a final show in August for the community.
