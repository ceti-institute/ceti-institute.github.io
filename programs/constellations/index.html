Collaborative Art-Tech Experiments for the Portland Winter Lights Festival

Weekend of Art-Tech Collaborative Creative Experiments:

CETI is convening a group of artists, technologists, architects, musicians, hackers, and engineers in January for a weekend of creative experiments at the CETI Lab at PSU - an exciting interdisciplinary creative jam to bring unique interactive light and sound pieces, immersive experiences, and performances to life.

Where:

At the CETI Lab in Portland State University's FMH Building, room 218. 1855 SW Broadway.

When: Jan 21 - 23, 2022

Constellations - a showcase and celebration of these magical interactive and immersive experiences will be shown as part of Portland's Winter Lights Festival 2022
