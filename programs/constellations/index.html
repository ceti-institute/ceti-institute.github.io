CETI Constellations

Shimmering Ecologies

In January 2022, a group of artists, technologists, architects, musicians, hackers, and engineers gathered for a weekend of Art-Tech Collaborative Creative Experiments at the CETI Lab at Portland State University — an exciting interdisciplinary creative jam to bring unique light and sound pieces, immersive experiences, and performances to life.

Constellations: Shimmering Ecologies is the result — these magical interactive and immersive installation experiences, shown as part of Portland's Winter Lights Festival Feb 5, 2022 in the FMH building at Portland State University.
