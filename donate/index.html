Who We Are

CETI (a Creative and Emergent Technology Institute) offers a vision for advanced lifelong learning, research, and professional development, supporting a diverse and interdisciplinary community of practitioners, professionals, educators, students, and researchers to successfully create the future. Through a carefully woven tapestry of workshops, institutes, participatory research, projects, convenings, and actions, we inspire and empower our community to create imaginative and impactful responses to emergent social and technological challenges.
