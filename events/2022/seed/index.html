Seed

CETI invites you to a wondrous experience in the Welcome Dome in Pioneer Courthouse Square focused on themes of spring and transformative rejuvenation.

Artists: Nanda D’agostino, Scott Garner, Scott Nieradka

Create/Production Team: Alanna Rise, Hilary Tsai, Miguel Higgins-Moy, Ayşe Gürsöz, Sean Light


Efflorescence

The dome, acting as an enormous seed in the square, will house a mesmerizing and meditative video experience drawing upon botanical phenomena and responsive to the presence of visitors.

This work is best experienced at dusk or evening. It is interactive/responsive to movement, gesture, and sound so feel free to perform!


Let Us Cultivate Our Garden

Externally, the seed blossoms into a massive floral sculpture through a web-based augmented reality layer encouraging and incorporating interaction with visitors and their mobile devices.

This augmented reality app is best experienced by daylight. Bring your smart phone!
