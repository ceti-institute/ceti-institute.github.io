CETI Tech Lab

The CETI Community Technology Lab at PSU is located at 1855 SW Broadway (FMH), Rm 218.

The lab provides access to cutting-edge technology, tools, support, and expertise to create, experiment, prototype, fabricate, innovate, and play with emerging technologies and media. The lab serves as an incubator and catalyst for participatory cross-disciplinary learning, teaching, mentoring, and creation, and for a critical engagement with technology and the role it plays in our ubiquitously connected world.
