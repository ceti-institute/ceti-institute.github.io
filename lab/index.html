CETI Lab

CETI workshops, trainings, and institutes offer opportunities for lifelong learning, professional development, skill-building, and career transformation. Our approach is participatory, inclusive, joyful, critical, and intersectional. We enthusiastically welcome a diverse motivated community, who are excited about relevant, impactful learning experiences in emerging technologies, and who are collaborative and generous in the engagement - willing to learn with and from, and willing to share experiences, expertise, and imagination.

More details at: eventbrite page and on the CETI calendar.
