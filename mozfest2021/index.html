How to Get Around in Mozilla Hubs

  • W & S keys move forward-back
  • A & D keys move left-right
  • Q & E keys or cursor for rotation
  • holding SHIFT key makes you move faster
  • holding down SPACEBAR will show you teleportation spots in some rooms
  • Press G key to enable fly mode, then point up and go forward using W or arrow key.
  • New to Mozilla Hubs? Watch this quick tutorial on how to get around or read Marin's Tips for Using Mozilla Hubs
