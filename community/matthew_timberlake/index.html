Matthew Timberlake

Board Member

Evangelist for digital equity and inclusion, and experienced leader of technical teams that have delivered transformative products. Matt has played a key role in nationally-recognized digital equity and inclusion programs.

As a technologist, Matt has a history of helping enterprises change how they use technology. He has led technology programs with budgets up to $34M, and has been a founder or early member of several startups, including one that developed into a leading software company with a multi-billion dollar valuation. He is passionately interested in new technology ventures that move us toward social justice, particularly digital equity and inclusion, net neutrality, and new approaches to education.
