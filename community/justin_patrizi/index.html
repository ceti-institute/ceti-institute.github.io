Justin Patrizi

Creative Technologist

Justin Patrizi is a creative technologist and mixed media artist. His works synthesize human interaction, data, and design into immersive pieces that explore the possibilities of the future with a range of influences from science fiction to street art. He is Interested in connecting human interaction with multi-sensory stimuli, using engaging and intuitive interfaces to create unique experiences. He is interested in harnessing rapidly advancing technology to democratize access to creative and educational mediums. He is frequently found in makerspaces, teaching workshops, or playing synthesizers in the Portland, Oregon area.
