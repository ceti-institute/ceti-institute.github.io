Marin Vesely

Creative Technologist

Marin Vesely is an interaction designer and creative researcher working at the intersection of art, technology, and activism. Marin has directed and led projects and hackathons related to creative technology in collaboration with CETI, Make + Think + Code Lab, Eyebeam, Food & Water Watch and with overseas organizations including O Sítio Art e Tecnologia in Florianópolis, Brazil and the Bergen Centre for Electronic Arts in Bergen, Norway. A large portion of Marin’s work is based in grassroots organizing and community led actions. She believes that equity and access are the most important aspects of environmental initiatives, and always centers the voice of marginalized community members. Marin’s goal is to use design, storytelling and compassion to generate global connectedness and collaboration in order to challenge injustices and fight for liberation.
