CETI

A Creative and Emergent Technology Institute

CETI is a model for advanced/professional education, research, and innovation in creative and emergent technology and design. Through a carefully woven tapestry of workshops, participatory research projects, creative experiments, and collaborative convenings, actions, and conversations, we empower and inspire a diverse community across sectors, disciplines, experiences, and expertise to create imaginative, insightful, and impactful responses to emergent social, environmental, and technological challenges.

CETI offers a vision for lifelong learning, professional development, transformation. We support a diverse and inclusive community of professionals, educators, students, entrepreneurs, researchers, and practitioners to successfully engage with and create the future.
